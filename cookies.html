🍪 Cookies Policy — nourishing transparency

Just like compost enriches the soil, small data bits help us cultivate a better experience. Learn how we use cookies and how you tend your own digital garden.

🌾 Our gentle approach to cookies

At Quorena Root Haven (“we,” “us,” or “our”), we believe in honest, earth-friendly practices — both in the garden and online. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them on our website, and the choices you have to manage your own digital footprint. We respect your privacy like we respect heirloom seeds: carefully, with intention, and without exploitation.

🌿 In a nutshell: We use only essential and functional cookies to keep the site running smoothly, plus a few analytical “crumbs” to understand how visitors interact with our pages. We never sell your data or use invasive tracking. Your trust is the fertile ground of our community.

🍪 What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences (such as language or region) over a period of time, so you don’t have to keep re-entering them. Think of them as little plant markers that help us navigate the rows of our online garden.

🕊️ Your choices — tending your own soil

You are always in control of your digital roots. When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can accept or adjust your cookie preferences. You can also manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Here's how:

  • Browser controls: Most web browsers allow you to block or delete cookies via their settings. Look for “privacy” or “site settings” in your browser menu.
  • Opt-out of analytics: You can prevent Google Analytics from using your data by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Withdraw consent: You may change your cookie preferences anytime by clicking the “Cookie Settings” link in the footer of any page, or by clearing cookies from your device.

Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of certain features (like submitting contact forms or viewing workshop calendars). However, we support your right to a private digital sanctuary.
